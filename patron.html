Patron

Silver

Tasting Panel Rating: 74

The aroma lean towards powdered sugar territory, but doesn't overwhelm. Also present is a small dose of green agave leaves. The palate, while clean, is sharp and a touch sweet, but not very plush. There is little in the middle in the way of flavor. As you continue to drink, you'll discover a bit more in the aroma by way of lime pith and chalk which is also what you are left with on the finish

Reposado

Tasting Panel Rating: 60

The sweet agave notes you found on the Silver Roca from Patrón are tamed here with a dash of cinnamon. The wood influence is such that you can still taste the agave, it hasn't been covered up. There is some slight slickness of vanilla on your palate with an ever so gentle hint of smoke from the barrel. The finish is bittersweet and you are left with just a mild tingling sensation on your gums.

Anejo

Tasting Panel Rating: 68

The añejo tequila has a pleasant amount of baking spices on your palate along with maple syrup and vanilla. The agave notes come through on the palate and the tequila has a rich, full quality in your mouth.
